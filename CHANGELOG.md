## [1.0.1] - 2024-12-26
### Added
- Swipe-to-skip gesture for Due Now medications
- Notification tap navigates to dashboard and refreshes medications
- Auto-hiding scrollbar in medication add dialog

### Improved
- Text truncation for long medication names on dashboard

## [1.0.0] - 2024-12-09
### Added
- Initial release
- Medication tracking with scheduled reminders
- Condition management with ICD-10 codes
- Dashboard with today's medications view
- Adherence tracking and statistics
- PRN (as-needed) medication support
- Profile with XP and streak tracking
- Dark mode support
