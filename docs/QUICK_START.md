# ComplyHealth Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with ComplyHealth in just 5 minutes!

---

## Step 1: Add Your First Condition (2 minutes)

1. Open ComplyHealth
2. Tap the **Health** icon (🏥) in the bottom navigation
3. Tap **Browse All** at the top
4. Use the search bar or browse categories
5. Find your condition and tap the **+ button**
6. Your condition is now added!

**Example:** Search for "diabetes" → Select "Type 2 diabetes mellitus" → Tap +

---

## Step 2: Add Your First Medication (2 minutes)

1. Tap the **Medications** icon (💊) in the bottom navigation
2. Tap the **+ button** (floating button, bottom right)
3. Fill in the medication details:
   - **Name**: Enter medication name (e.g., "Metformin")
   - **Dosage**: Enter strength (e.g., "500mg")
   - **Condition**: Select the condition you just added
   - **Type**: Choose Scheduled or PRN

4. **If Scheduled:**
   - Select number of times per day
   - Set times (e.g., 8:00 AM, 8:00 PM)

5. **If PRN (as-needed):**
   - Enter max daily doses (e.g., 6)

6. Tap **Add Medication**

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile (1 minute)

1. Tap the **Profile** icon (👤) in the bottom navigation
2. Tap the **Edit button** (pencil icon)
3. Fill in your information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Date of Birth
   - Allergies (important!)
4. Tap **Save**

---

## Step 4: Start Tracking! (Daily)

1. Return to the **Dashboard** (home icon)
2. View your medication schedule for today
3. When you take a dose, **tap the checkbox** next to it
4. Watch your adherence streak grow!

---

## Pro Tips for Day One

✅ **Set realistic medication times** - Choose times you can stick to consistently

✅ **Add all your medications** - The more complete your list, the better your tracking

✅ **Log doses immediately** - Make it a habit to check off doses right after taking them

✅ **Explore condition details** - Tap on any condition to learn more about it

✅ **Check adherence history** - View your medication-taking patterns over time
