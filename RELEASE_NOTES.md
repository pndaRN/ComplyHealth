# SmartPatient Release Notes

## Version 1.0.0 - First Release

**Release Date**: December 9, 2024

We're excited to introduce SmartPatient, your personal health companion for tracking chronic conditions and managing medications with confidence.

---

## What's New

### Medication Management
- **Smart Medication Tracking**: Keep track of all your medications in one place with detailed dosing information
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Set up regular medication schedules or track as-needed (PRN) medications
- **Daily Reminders**: Never miss a dose with customizable notification reminders
- **Adherence Tracking**: Visual 30-day adherence history helps you see your medication-taking patterns at a glance
- **PRN Dose Counting**: For as-needed medications, track daily doses with color-coded warnings to help you stay within safe limits
- **Multiple Sorting Options**: View your medications by name, condition, or due time - whatever works best for you

### Condition Tracking
- **Comprehensive Database**: Browse and add from over 500 chronic conditions organized by medical category
- **ICD-10 Coded**: All conditions use standard medical coding (ICD-10) for accuracy
- **Smart Search**: Find conditions quickly by name, common name, or medical code
- **Custom Conditions**: Add conditions not in our database to fit your unique health needs
- **Request Missing Conditions**: Help us improve by reporting conditions you'd like to see added

### Health Insights
- **Dashboard Overview**: See your day at a glance with today's medications and adherence summary
- **At-A-Glance Widget**: Quick view of all your conditions and their medications
- **Personal Profile**: Track your health information, allergies, and view your adherence metrics

### Sharing & Export
- **PDF Export**: Generate professional medication reports to share with your healthcare providers
- **Data Backup**: Export your complete health data as a backup file
- **Easy Sharing**: Share reports and backups directly from the app

### Personalization
- **Theme Options**: Choose light mode, dark mode, or automatic based on your device settings
- **Customizable Experience**: Organize your medications and conditions the way that makes sense to you

### Privacy & Security
- **Local Storage**: Your health data stays on your device - we don't store it on our servers
- **No Account Required**: Start using the app immediately without creating an account or sharing personal information
- **Crash Reporting**: Anonymous crash reports help us improve the app (no health data included)

### Easy Onboarding
- **Interactive Tutorial**: Get started quickly with our step-by-step onboarding guide
- **Skip Anytime**: Already familiar? Skip ahead and start tracking right away

### Feedback System
- **In-App Feedback**: Report bugs, request features, or share general feedback directly from the app

---

## Getting Started

1. **Launch the app** and complete the optional onboarding tutorial
2. **Add your conditions** from the Health tab - search or browse our database
3. **Add your medications** from the Medications tab - link them to your conditions
4. **Set up reminders** in Settings to get notified when it's time to take your medications
5. **Log your doses** daily using the dashboard checklist or medication cards

## Important Information

### Known Limitations

**Local Storage Only**
- Your data is stored only on your device
- We recommend regularly exporting backups (Settings → Export Data)
- Uninstalling the app will delete your data unless you have a backup
- Data cannot currently be synced across multiple devices

**Manual Medication Entry**
- You'll need to enter medication names and doses manually
- The app does not verify medication information or check for interactions
- Always consult your healthcare provider about your medications

**Beta Software**
- This is our first public release - you may encounter occasional bugs
- We appreciate your feedback to help us improve
- Use the in-app feedback feature to report issues

**Not a Substitute for Medical Advice**
- SmartPatient is a tracking tool, not a medical device
- Always consult healthcare professionals for medical decisions
- Do not use this app for emergency situations
