# SmartPatient v1.0.0 - First Release

We're excited to introduce **SmartPatient**, your personal health companion for tracking chronic conditions and managing medications with confidence.

## Highlights

**Medication Management**
- Track all your medications with flexible scheduling (regular or as-needed)
- Daily reminders to never miss a dose
- Visual 30-day adherence tracking
- PRN dose counting with color-coded safety warnings
- Sort medications by name, condition, or due time

**Condition Tracking**
- Browse over 500 chronic conditions with ICD-10 coding
- Smart search by name or medical code
- Add custom conditions
- Request missing conditions to help us improve

**Health Insights**
- Dashboard with daily medication checklist
- At-a-glance summary of all conditions and medications
- Personal profile with adherence metrics

**Sharing & Privacy**
- Export professional PDF reports for healthcare providers
- Backup your data anytime
- Local storage - your data stays on your device
- No account required

**Personalization**
- Light, dark, or automatic theme modes
- Customizable medication organization
- Interactive onboarding tutorial

## Getting Started

1. Launch the app and complete the optional tutorial
2. Add your conditions from the Health tab
3. Add your medications and link them to conditions
4. Enable reminders in Settings
5. Log your doses daily from the dashboard

## Important Notes

### Known Limitations

**Local Storage Only**
- Data stored only on your device - we recommend regular backups (Settings → Export Data)
- Uninstalling removes all data unless backed up
- No cross-device sync yet (coming soon!)

**Manual Entry Required**
- Medication names and doses must be entered manually
- App does not verify medication information or check for interactions
- Always consult your healthcare provider

**Beta Software**
- First public release - you may encounter occasional bugs
- Please report issues using the in-app feedback feature
- Not a substitute for medical advice or emergency care
